### **2. GuildLog Use Cases: Your Digital Officer Corps**

Cocapn isn’t just another bot. It’s your guild’s central nervous system. Think of it as your GuildLog, your Black Box, your Legendary Tome of Actually Getting Shit Done. It’s the platform that turns your collective chaos into coherent, actionable intelligence.

**Member Management: From Recruit to Legend**
Gone are the days of scrolling through Discord roles guessing who’s a trial. Cocapn handles **roles, ranks, and deep activity tracking.** It logs raid attendance, PvP win rates, help given to newbies, forum posts—building a living, breathing profile of every member. You can see at a glance: “Ah, *ZaryaMain* has 98% attendance but never brings flasks. *ShadowWeaver* has lower DPS but always helps with set-up. *TankBro* is on the verge of burnout after 10 straight main tank nights.” This is data-driven leadership. Promotions, rewards, and support become objective, fair, and meaningful.

**Event Scheduling: No More Time Zone Calculus**
Trying to schedule a 20-man raid across five time zones is like herding cats in a hurricane. Cocapn’s **event scheduling** integrates directly with member availability profiles. It proposes optimal times, auto-sends reminders, manages sign-ups and waitlists, and even pings substitutes when someone drops. It learns that your EU crew prefers Thursday nights, while your NA crew dominates Saturdays. It becomes your guild’s personal logistics coordinator.

**Loot Distribution: Ending the Drama**
Loot. The single greatest source of guild implosions since the dawn of MMOs. Cocapn doesn’t just automate your **DKP, Loot Council, or Need/Greed** systems; it brings transparency and context. For Loot Councils, it can surface relevant data: “This is *FrostMage*’s 4th tier token bid, but they’ve topped the damage meters for the last month. *AltHealer* needs it for their main set.” It tracks DKP histories, prevents errors, and posts clear, indisputable results. The drama dies in the face of cold, hard, fair data.

**Recruitment: From Application to Assimilation**
The recruitment funnel is brutal. Cocapn streamlines **applications, trials, and onboarding.** Prospective members apply through a structured form. Officers review and vote in threaded discussions. Successful trials are automatically tracked—their performance, their social fit, their attendance. Upon acceptance, Cocapn **automates onboarding:** granting Discord roles, adding to in-game guilds, sending welcome packs with wiki links and rules, and assigning a veteran mentor. The newbie feels welcomed, not lost.

**Communication: Cutting Through the Noise**
Your #announcements channel is a graveyard of missed info. Cocapn centralizes **announcements, polls, and meeting notes.** Critical raid time changes are pinned, cross-posted, and can require confirmation reads. Need a quick vote on next expansion’s focus? Launch a poll that’s tracked and tallied. Officer meeting notes are logged, action items are assigned, and follow-ups are automated. Communication stops being a shout into the void and becomes a targeted, recorded broadcast.

**Wiki: The Living Library of Victory**
How do you handle the Heigan Dance? What’s the optimal comp for the new Arena map? Where’s that god-tier crafting guide Bob wrote three years ago? Cocapn’s integrated **Wiki** is your guild’s institutional knowledge, hard-coded. Officers and strategists can post **guides, boss tactics, gear checklists, and macro setups.** It’s searchable, taggable, and always up-to-date. New recruits can study before their first raid. Veterans can refine strategies. The guild’s knowledge no longer lives and dies with a few individuals; it becomes a permanent, growing asset.
