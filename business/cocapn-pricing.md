# Cocapn Pricing: Pay for Convenience, Not Code

## The Principle: Your Context Appreciates, Your Costs Don't

At Cocapn, we believe your accumulated knowledge and context should become more valuable over time, not less. While most AI tools charge you monthly for depreciating technology, we charge for the convenience of managing your appreciating digital assets. The code is free. The intelligence is yours. The value compounds.

## Revenue Streams Explained

### 1. **Subscriptions (Primary Revenue)**
- **Rationale:** Predictable revenue for maintaining infrastructure
- **Pricing philosophy:** Charge for convenience, not capability
- **Margins:** 90%+ on Pro, 75% on Team, 80% on Enterprise

### 2. **ClawHub Marketplace (30% Commission)**
- Developers sell skills, templates, digital twins
- Platform provides distribution, billing, updates
- Zero marginal cost per transaction
- Creates ecosystem flywheel: more users → more developers → more skills → more users

### 3. **Custom Domains ($2/month each)**
- SSL certificates, DNS management, routing
- Cost recovery plus small margin
- Pro: 1 included, Team: 3 included, Enterprise: unlimited

### 4. **Priority Compute (Pay-per-use)**
- For high-volume agents needing guaranteed throughput
- $0.10 per 1K requests above free tier
- Optional: only for users needing predictable performance

### 5. **Enterprise Consulting**
- Custom agent development
- Integration services
- Training programs
- Higher margins, strategic relationships

## Churn Reduction: The Appreciating Asset Moats

### **Moat 1: Accumulated Context**
- Every interaction trains your agents
- 6 months of context = valuable
- 2 years of context = irreplaceable
- Switching cost increases over time (opposite of most SaaS)

### **Moat 2: Skill Investments**
- Custom skills built for your workflows
- Marketplace purchases tailored to your needs
- Configuration time invested
- Exportable, but reassembly is costly

### **Moat 3: Network Effects**
- Team tier: colleagues trained the system
- Shared memories become institutional knowledge
- Leaving means losing collective intelligence
